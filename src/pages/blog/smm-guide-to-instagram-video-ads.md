## How to Be Successful with Instagram Video Ads

Nearly [90% of marketers](https://softcube.com/70-video-marketing-statistics-for-2020/) claim that video ads on Instagram have been successful for them. Obviously, the question is _How do these marketers succeed?_

As a social media platform, Instagram was created to focus viewers’ attention on visual content.

And when it comes to visuals, video is king. That’s why the majority of [Instagram ad formats](https://softcube.com/instagram-ad-formats-basics-and-recommendations/) support video content. 

Let’s recall those formats and mention some Instagram video ad specs:

### Instagram Video Ads

https://giphy.com/gifs/Rl6KH5X1SrlYFBU2ip

- Instagram video ad size: up to 30MB
- Instagram video ad length: up to 2 minutes

Traditional video ads on Instagram have two formats: landscape and square.

### Instagram Stories Ads

https://giphy.com/gifs/m93R2sZTshqOOwzXYf

- Instagram Story video ad length: up to 2 minutes
- Instagram Story video ad size: up to 30MB

Instagrammers adore watching Stories. According to Statista, nearly 35% of [all sponsored content](https://www.statista.com/statistics/986026/instagram-sponsored-content-by-type/) on Instagram is in the form of a Story.

As a marketer, Stories not only let you reach a huge audience but give you limitless possibilities to tell about your business in any way you want.

To learn how to do that, read our [Instagram Stories ads](https://softcube.com/the-complete-guide-to-instagram-stories-ads/) tutorial.

### Instagram Explore Ads

https://giphy.com/gifs/egAb7wA5y9gyI5XKgr

- Instagram Explore video ad size: up to 30MB
- Instagram Explore video ad length: up to 2 minutes

People are always looking for new things, especially on Instagram. Explore ads help users fulfill their desire for discovery while allowing businesses to reach potential customers who are likely to buy their products and services.

### Instagram Carousel Ads

https://giphy.com/gifs/jTAZDxvrW8ddcO60nY

- Instagram carousel video ad size: up to 30 MB
- Instagram carousel video ad length: up to 15 seconds

You can create a few ads in one thanks to the cards available in the carousel ad format. Each card is a separate item in your carousel where you can add a link, visual, and text.

Keep in mind that [different ad placements](https://softcube.com/how-to-place-ads-on-facebook-ad-placements/) support different numbers of cards. For example, you can use up to 10 cards for Instagram Feed carousels and expandable Stories carousels, but you can use only three cards for native Stories carousels.

## 7 Tips and Tricks for Creating Amazing Video Ads on Instagram

Here are Instagram video ads best practices for making spectacular content that will impress Instagram users and make them convert into customers.

### Try Instagram Mobile Apps

First of all, don’t forget that there are creative tools like [Softcube artificial intelligence](http://softcube.com) that can simplify the editing process and make your videos more attractive.

![Instagram video ads — mobile apps](/img/instagram-video-ads-mobile-apps.jpg)

Additionally, you can try two official mobile apps developed for Instagram advertisers: Boomerang and Hyperlapse. Interestingly, [25% of videos](https://softcube.com/70-video-marketing-statistics-for-2020/) shared on Instagram are created with the Boomerang app.

### Surprise Viewers in the First 4 Seconds

Keep in mind that you’re going to create videos for mobile users. Your video ads should be as short as possible to catch the attention of your target audience and encourage them to become your customers.

https://giphy.com/gifs/giRYBoxvYkiEyQfDL3

### Focus on Your Business

Add visual elements and use editing techniques to come up with a unique promotional style for your company or brand and stand out from the crowd of businesses and creators on Instagram.

### Use Lights and Show Action

Lights, camera, action. These three words mean a lot not only in Hollywood but also in Instagram video marketing.

You can effectively influence your viewers’ perceptions by showing your business, products, and services in action. Always use good lighting to make your content vivid and visually appealing.

### Experiment with Very Short Videos

Every day, the average Instagram user scrolls a few miles on the Instagram feed and makes a few hundred taps on the screen.

https://giphy.com/gifs/XHXCXmHJFAMpwGQR44

One of the best ways to increase your customer base on Instagram is to appreciate the time of your potential customers who take part in this marathon. Test several-second videos. They’re instant and informative.

### Add Polling Stickers

In 9 out of 10 beta video ad campaigns, polling stickers help achieve a [20% lower cost per video view](https://business.instagram.com/blog/bringing-interactivity-to-instagram-stories-ads/) for three-second videos. Not to mention that it’s a great way to find out the interests and habits of your audience.

https://giphy.com/gifs/igV317AUzip8oNRork

### Post More

According to Instagram Business, [45% of Instagrammers](https://softcube.com/70-video-marketing-statistics-for-2020/) think they’ll watch more video content on mobile in the future. Promotional videos are most effective when posted a few times a week or more.
